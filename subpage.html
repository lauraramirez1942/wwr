River Route 1

  • Route Name: Thunder Rapids
  • Difficulty: Class IV
  • Length: 12 miles
  • Duration: 4 hours
  • Start Point: Pine Valley
  • End Point: Riverside Campground
  • Scenic Features: Tall cliffs, dense forests, and wildlife sightings
  • Highlights: Big Drop (Class V rapid), Eagle's Nest (scenic viewpoint)
  • Recommended Season: Spring to early summer
  • Required Skill Level: Intermediate to advanced
  • Equipment Needed: Raft, helmet, life jacket, paddle
  • Guided Tours: Available
  • Local Guides: Pine Valley Adventure Tours
  • Permits: Required; obtainable at Pine Valley Ranger Station
  • Safety Notes: Follow the guide's instructions, be aware of potential hazards, and stay together as a group.

River Route 2

  • Route Name: Crystal Cascade
  • Difficulty: Class III+
  • Length: 10 miles
  • Duration: 5 hours
  • Start Point: Misty Meadows
  • End Point: Whispering Pines
  • Scenic Features: Glistening waterfalls, towering cliffs, and serene meadows
  • Highlights: Thunderous Plunge (Class IV rapid), Serenity Point (tranquil picnic area)
  • Recommended Season: Late spring to early autumn
  • Required Skill Level: Intermediate to advanced
  • Equipment Needed: Canoe, helmet, life jacket, paddle
  • Guided Tours: Available
  • Local Guides: Misty Meadows Adventure Tours
  • Permits: Required; obtainable at Whispering Pines Ranger Station
  • Safety Notes: Pay attention to the guide's instructions, watch out for submerged rocks, and maintain group cohesion at all times.

River Route 3

  • Route Name: Wildwater Run
  • Difficulty: Class III+
  • Length: 8 miles
  • Duration: 3 hours
  • Start Point: River's Edge
  • End Point: Lakeside Camp
  • Scenic Features: Majestic waterfalls, lush greenery, and abundant wildlife
  • Highlights: Rushing Rapids (Class IV rapid), Serene Cove (peaceful resting spot)
  • Recommended Season: Summer to early fall
  • Required Skill Level: Intermediate to advanced
  • Equipment Needed: Kayak, helmet, life jacket, paddle
  • Guided Tours: Available
  • Local Guides: River's Edge Adventure Tours
  • Permits: Required; obtainable at Lakeside Ranger Station
  • Safety Notes: Follow the guide's instructions, stay alert to potential obstacles, and maintain communication within the group.
